Fishing

Fishing is one of my favorite hobbies!

Why do I go fishing?

Fishing is one of my favorite ways to relax and connect with nature. It can be very time consuming and requires a lot of patience, but catching a fish is always so rewarding!

Fishing techniques

  1. Bait
    • Spinners: Throw and slowly reel back. Great for catching trout and bass.
    • Rapalas: Great for catching aggressive fish like pike. Try to mimic fish movements when reeling in.
    • Power bait: Toss it out there and let it sit until something bites.
  2. Casting Tips
    • 1. Draw the rod back, and release the string as you thrust forward.
    • 2. Cast in areas with weeds, rocks, branches, or somewhere a fish would like to hide.
    • 3. Be aware of surroundings and avoid snagging a tree or a person passing by.
  3. Catching a fish
    • Releasing: If you plan to return the fish, keep it in the water as long as possible and handle it gently.
    • Preparing: If you want to eat the fish, put a knife through the top of the head to minimize pain. Set it on ice so it doesn't go bad.
